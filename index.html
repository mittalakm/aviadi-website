About

Ashok Mittal

MD & CEO, BillMart FinTech  ·  Director, FynX Capital  ·  Founder, Prest Loans

For over 30 years, Ashok Mittal has worked across India’s Capital Markets, Banking, and Financial Services — from equities, commodities and forex to building digital lending businesses from the ground up.

In 2017, he acquired an NBFC and rebranded it as Prest Loans, with a simple mission: power small businesses with fast, flexible, and accessible credit. Today, as MD & CEO of BillMart FinTech, he leads a platform helping MSMEs and corporates unlock working capital through supply chain finance, bill discounting, and gig-worker lending solutions — alongside his role as Director at FynX Capital, focused on MSME and personal loans.

A passionate advocate for financial inclusion, he builds the technology, teams, and partnerships that bring credit to underserved businesses across India. He is the author of “The Fintech Entrepreneur’s Guide” and a regular speaker at industry platforms including Assocham, FICCI, Convergence India Expo, the NBFC & FinTech Conclave, B2B Infomedia, and various MSME growth forums. He has been recognised by prominent national figures including Shri Narendra Modi, Shri Amit Shah, and Shri Pranab Mukherjee.
