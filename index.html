About

Ashok Mittal

MD & CEO, BillMart FinTech  ·  Director, FynX Capital  ·  Founder, PrestLoans

For over 30 years, Ashok Mittal has worked across India’s Capital Markets, Banking, Financial Services and Forex — from equities and commodities to building digital lending businesses from the ground up.

In 2017, he acquired an NBFC and rebranded it as PrestLoans, a new-age FinTech NBFC offering working capital, MSME, invoice / inventory and EV loans. Today, as MD & CEO of BillMart FinTech, he leads a platform helping MSMEs and corporates unlock working capital through supply chain finance, bill discounting, and gig-worker lending — alongside his role as Director at FynX Capital, focused on MSME and personal loans.

A passionate advocate for financial inclusion, he is a Harvard Business School executive programme alumnus who has delivered 200+ seminars and authored “The Fintech Entrepreneur’s Guide” (BPB Publications). He speaks regularly at FICCI, ASSOCHAM, PHD Chamber, ICFAI, and the NBFC & FinTech Conclave, and has been honoured by national figures including Shri Narendra Modi, Shri Amit Shah, and Shri Pranab Mukherjee.
